_High-Level Data Link Control_ is a communication protocol used for **transmitting data** between devices in **telecommunication/networking**.

> [!info]
> The primary purpose of _High-Level Data Link Control_ is to ensure reliable data transfer, even without a continuous connection.

It provides various operations such as:
- [[Packetizing]]
- [[Addressing]]
- [[Error Detection]]
- [[Error Correction]]
- [[Error Control]]
- [[Flow Control]]
