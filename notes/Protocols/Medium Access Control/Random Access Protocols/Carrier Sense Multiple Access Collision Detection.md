_[[Carrier Sense Multiple Access]] Collision Detection_ is a variant of [[Carrier Sense Multiple Access|carrier sense multiple access]] in which potential collisions are detected by monitoring the **transmission medium** after a frame has been sent by waiting for an acknowledgement, similar to [[Pure ALOHA|ALOHA]].
